About us

BMM was established as a business entity in 1930.We are committed to shaping the future landscape of sustainable transport and infrastructure solutions.

BMM will lead the way to the future of mobility and comfort all around the world with the safest and most responsibe way.Our commitment to quality and constant development, we aim to exceed expectations of our costumers.Our aim is always to add value.We are one of the world's leading manufactures of luxury automobiles, and we provide premium services. We will meet challenging goals by engaging the talent and passion of people, who belive there is always a better way.Toward enriching communities and achieving their sustainable development, BMM is advancing it is social contribution activities by working together with a wide range of various levels of society, while putting available resources to the most-effective use possible.
